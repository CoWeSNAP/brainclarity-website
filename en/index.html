The eight games

Each game trains a different cognitive domain. Five difficulty levels per game.

N-Back

The classic working-memory challenge. Spot when a stimulus matches one shown N steps ago.

Dual N-Back

Same idea, twice the load — visual and audio simultaneously. The boss level.

Serial Recall

Watch a sequence, type it back — exactly in order.

Digit Span

How many numbers can you hold at once? Gets longer until you hit your capacity.

Spatial Memory

Remember and reproduce positions on a grid. Visuospatial.

Stroop Test

The words lie. Trust the ink. Attention and inhibition.

Pattern Match

Spot what changed. Fast visual comparison.

Speed Match

Does this card match the previous one? Pure processing speed.

Frequently asked questions

Is Brain Clarity a medical product?

No. Brain Clarity is an app for personal cognitive training and entertainment. It is not a medical device, not a diagnostic tool, and not a substitute for medical care. If you have a medical concern about your memory or cognitive health, please talk to a doctor.

Which devices does Brain Clarity run on?

Brain Clarity launches first on iOS (iPhone, iPad) with Android following shortly after. It's a native mobile app — no browser version planned.

How much does the subscription cost and how do I cancel?

Brain Clarity Pro is $7.99/month or $39.99/year (~$3.33/month effective). The annual plan includes a 7-day free trial. Cancellation works directly through your Apple ID or Google Account settings — anytime, no notice period beyond what the law requires.

What data do you collect?

All we need for sign-up is an email address. We record your game scores so you can see your stats, plus optional profile data you provide yourself (age range, education). No IP addresses, no location, no advertising IDs, no tracking pixels. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Can I export or delete my data?

Yes, both with a single tap in the app. In Profile settings you can download all your data as a JSON file (Art. 20 GDPR) or permanently delete your account along with all game data (Art. 17 GDPR).

Will this actually improve my real-world cognition?

What the app measures is your performance in the games it contains — and that does improve with practice. Whether and how much that transfers to everyday tasks (focus in meetings, recall, multitasking) is not scientifically settled. We make no medical promises — we give you transparent data on your in-game training progress.

Who's behind Brain Clarity?

Brain Clarity is built by snap DISCOVERY AG out of Bochum, Germany. snap GmbH (also in Bochum) handles billing for Brain Clarity Pro. Both are fully identified in the Legal Notice.
